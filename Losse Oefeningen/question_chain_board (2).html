🔗 Question Chain

How the question chain works

  1. A says a sentence: "Ik werk bij Westport."
  2. B asks a question — about the answer or something new.
  3. A answers in a full sentence.
  4. C asks the next question. Don't stop!
A Ik werk bij Westport.
B Waar is Westport? (open · same)
A Westport is in Eindhoven.
C Hoe laat begin je? (open · new)
A Ik begin om 7 uur.
B Begin je altijd om 7 uur? (closed · same)
… keeps going!
